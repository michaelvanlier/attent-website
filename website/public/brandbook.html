09 / Voice

Tone of Voice

The cheeky accountability partner. Warm, direct, and caring. Never preachy, never corporate.

Voice Traits

Cheeky
not mean
Casual
not sloppy
Direct
not cold
Caring
not preachy

Cheek Levels

Low cheek (settings, errors) Medium (onboarding) HIGH (landing page)

Writing Rules

Do
  • Sentence case everywhere
  • Contractions always (you're, it's, don't)
  • Short sentences. Punchy.
  • Say "your people", "loved ones"
  • Say "show up", "show love"
  • Active voice
Don't
  • No exclamation marks
  • No emoji in copy
  • Never say "your network"
  • Never say "stay connected"
  • Never say "nudge"
  • No passive voice or corporate speak

Copy Examples

Stay connected with your professional network through intelligent nudges.
Show up for your people. Before you forget.
Attent helps you maintain your relationships by sending you reminders!
Life gets busy. Your people still matter. We'll help you remember that.
You haven't engaged with this contact in 30 days. Reconnect now!
Hey, you haven't talked to Mum in a while. Just saying.
